Part of revolutionizing school uniforms

But wait, let's rewind a bit! Before my time at Open Weaver, I was weaving a different kind of magic at Scribbles, a school uniform manufacturer. For two years, I worked on conceptualizing and refining school uniform designs. Here, I discovered a passion for the intersection of aesthetics and functionality. This role gave me a solid foundation in understanding user needs and preferences.

A Pause and a Pivot

Then came a plot twist—my career break for marriage and pregnancy. As a first-time mom, life felt like a whirlwind. Amidst sleepless nights and diaper changes, I discovered my knack for organizing chaos. Keeping baby necessities within reach, arranging my space for efficiency, and finding clever ways to keep my little one engaged—all these everyday challenges turned into a playground for my design skills. This break revealed that design is everywhere, reigniting my passion for creating solutions that make life easier.
